Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 23, 2026

We respect your right to control your personal information. This page explains how to request deletion of data we hold about you.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to info@peekskilltree.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request and include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address or phone number associated with your account
  • A brief statement confirming you want your data deleted

📧 Send Deletion Request

What Gets Deleted

  • Your contact information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Service request history and notes
  • Quotes, invoices, and job records (see exceptions below)
  • Photos and videos you uploaded
  • Any messages we exchanged

What We May Need to Retain

Some data we're legally required to keep:

  • Tax records — invoices and payments must be retained for 7 years (IRS and NY State).
  • Safety / insurance records — incident reports relating to insured work may be retained while relevant claims are open.
  • Backup archives — our system backups may contain data for up to 60 days after deletion, then are overwritten.

Retained records are isolated, not used for any marketing or outreach, and will be permanently deleted once the legal retention period ends.

Response Time

We respond to all deletion requests within 30 days. If we need more time (rare), we'll email you explaining why.

If You Connected Through Facebook or Instagram

If you contacted us through our Facebook or Instagram accounts, you can also remove our app's access to your data:

  1. Open Facebook Settings → Security and Login → Apps and Websites (or the equivalent on Instagram).
  2. Find the "Second Nature Tree Service" or "Branch Manager" entry.
  3. Click Remove. This revokes our access going forward.

Revoking access doesn't delete data we already have. To delete that data, also email us as described above.

If You Connected Through Google (Business Profile)

Manage connected apps at myaccount.google.com/permissions. Revoke Branch Manager there to cut off access, then email us to delete retained data.
