Tree-specific superpowers

Things Jobber will never build, because their customer isn't you

Branch Manager ships features that only matter to a tree company. Jobber serves 250,000 customers across 50 industries — they're not going to build dielectric-test reminders or DBH measurement.

🌳

AI tree species ID

Photo of a tree returns species + condition + suggested service in seconds. Pre-trained on the tree-care domain, runs on your phone.

📐

DBH from photo

Camera-based diameter-at-breast-height measurement. No tape measure, no climbing — just snap and the number is in the quote.

🎬

Walk-and-narrate quoting

Record a 60-second video of the property. AI extracts every tree, every condition, every recommended service. Quote line items auto-populate.

🪜

ANSI Z133 + A92.2 compliance

Annual climbing-gear inspections, dielectric tests, helmet retire-by dates. Required by federal law for insured tree work — built in, not bolted on.

🪓

Equipment OEM catalog

Stihl chains, Bandit knives, Kubota engine parts. One-tap deep-link to Bailey's, Sherrilltree, Stephenson Equipment. Zero "what part number was that?"

🪵

Chip drop network

Map of private drop spots closer than the municipal site. Capacity, accepted species, gate codes — crew picks the closest open spot on the way back.

Where Jobber is better — for now

Honest about the gaps

Branch Manager is newer and smaller. There are places Jobber's 10+ years of investment shows. We tell you up front.

Where Jobber wins today

  • QuickBooks Online native sync. Jobber pushes invoices, payments, and customer records to QBO automatically. Branch Manager exports CSV today; native sync is on the roadmap.
  • Direct ACH payments. Jobber's payment processor accepts bank-to-bank transfers natively (lower fees than card). Branch Manager processes ACH through Stripe (works, slightly slower).
  • Polished mobile app. Jobber has dedicated iOS/Android apps with years of UX polish. Branch Manager runs as a PWA + Capacitor wrapper — works well, but not as native-feeling.
  • Larger support team. Jobber has phone support and a big help center. Branch Manager support today is direct from the founder — faster on niche tree-service questions, slower on standard SaaS-y stuff.
  • Industry templates outside trees. If you're also doing landscaping, snow removal, or pest control, Jobber has those workflows out of the box. Branch Manager is laser-focused on tree work.
