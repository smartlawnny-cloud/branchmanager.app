Every part of the job, in one place.

From the first phone call to the paid invoice, Branch Manager replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, sticky notes, and apps that don't talk to each other.

📋

Quotes & Estimates

Build a quote on-site with line items, photos, and a property map. Send via email or SMS. Customer signs and approves online — no PDF round-trip.

🗓️

Scheduling & Dispatch

Drag-and-drop crew assignment, geofenced auto-arrive, day-before reminders to clients. Pre-trip inspections built in.

💵

Invoicing & Payments

Auto-generate invoices from completed jobs. Stripe checkout, deposits, payment plans, and tipping built in. Receipts go out instantly.

💬

Two-way SMS & Calls

Every call, text, and voicemail logged automatically. Inline action chips on inbound replies — one-tap accept a quote that says "YES."

🚛

Fleet & Equipment

Live truck positions, OBD-II diagnostics, maintenance reminders by mileage and engine hours. Manuals and part numbers per piece of equipment.

🔗

Customer Portal

Magic-link sign-in for your customers — they see all their invoices, quotes, job history, and photos in one place. They pay you faster.

Why Branch Manager

Because the existing options don't fit.

Generic field-service software is built for HVAC and plumbing. Tree work has its own rhythm — property maps with hazard markers, photo before/afters, equipment that costs more than a truck, and customers who want a Google review more than a paper invoice.

Built in production at a real tree service

Every feature you see is something an actual tree service company needed yesterday. No demo-ware. No "coming soon." If it's in the product, it's because we use it ourselves.

One tool, not five

Stop paying for Jobber + QuickBooks + Mailchimp + a separate scheduling tool + a separate invoicing tool. Branch Manager bundles all of it, with the integrations that actually matter.

Field-first, not desk-first

Quote on a phone. Sign a contract on a phone. Snap before/after photos that auto-link to the job. The web app and the iOS app share the same data — no double entry.

White-label ready

Multi-tenant from day one. If you have multiple service brands, or want to run a private-label version for your own crews, the plumbing is already there.
