Eight features that pull their weight

Every photo earns its place in your business — for proof, for marketing, for billing, for crew safety.

📍

GPS + timestamp burned in

Date, time, and lat/lng on every shot. Insurance-grade proof you were on-site.

🏷️

Smart tags

Before, After, Hazard, Damage, Equipment, Permit — or roll your own. Search across every job.

✎

Annotate on the spot

Circle hazards, draw arrows, add text. Crew sees what matters before they swing the saw.

🔗

Share without an account

One link, your client sees the gallery. Great for marketing — they share it themselves.

📄

Branded photo reports

One tap, print-ready PDF with your logo. Send to insurance, customers, or municipalities.

📡

Works offline

Shoot in the woods. Photos queue locally and upload the moment you have signal.

🎯

One-tap "new job here"

Tap the button, GPS fills the address, suggests an existing client, you're estimating in 10 seconds.

🌳

Tied to every record

Each photo lives on the right job, quote, or client. No "where did I save that?"
