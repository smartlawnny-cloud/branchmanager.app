Employee Handbook

Second Nature Tree Service LLC · Effective April 2026

Welcome

Welcome to Second Nature Tree Service. This handbook outlines the basic policies and expectations for all employees. It is not a contract of employment.

At-Will Employment

Employment with Second Nature Tree Service LLC is at-will. Either you or the company may end the employment relationship at any time, for any lawful reason, with or without cause or notice. Nothing in this handbook creates a contract or guarantee of employment.

Equal Employment

We provide equal employment opportunity regardless of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, veteran status, or any other protected characteristic under federal, state, or local law.

Work Schedule

  • Standard hours: Monday–Friday, 7:00 AM – 3:30 PM
  • Hours vary by season and weather. You will be notified of schedule changes via the crew group chat.
  • Be at the truck yard by call time. Late arrivals delay the entire crew.

Attendance

  • If you can't make it, call or text Doug by 6:00 AM.
  • No call / no show = written warning. Two = termination.
  • Weather delays will be communicated by 6:00 AM via group text.

Pay & Overtime

  • Pay period: Bi-weekly (every other Friday) via direct deposit through Gusto.
  • Overtime: Hours over 40 per week are paid at 1.5x your regular rate (NY Labor Law).
  • All overtime must be pre-approved by Doug.
  • Clock in/out using the Branch Manager app. Accurate time = accurate pay.

NY Meal & Rest Breaks

  • Shifts over 6 hours that extend over the noon meal period: 30-minute unpaid lunch break.
  • Shifts starting before 11 AM and extending past 7 PM: additional 20-minute break between 5–7 PM.
  • You may not skip breaks to leave early without written agreement.

Paid Time Off

  • PTO accrues based on tenure. Discuss your PTO balance with Doug.
  • Request time off at least 1 week in advance for planned absences.
  • NY Paid Sick Leave: You accrue 1 hour of sick leave for every 30 hours worked, up to 40 hours per year.

Safety

  • Safety is our #1 priority. No job is worth an injury.
  • Required PPE at all times: hard hat, safety glasses, ear protection, gloves, steel-toe boots.
  • Chainsaw chaps required when operating any chainsaw.
  • Fall protection required for all work above 6 feet.
  • Report all injuries, near-misses, and hazards to your crew lead immediately.
  • You have the right and duty to stop work if you see an unsafe condition.

Sexual Harassment Prevention

Second Nature Tree Service has zero tolerance for sexual harassment. All employees must complete the NY State sexual harassment prevention training annually. Report any harassment to Doug Brown immediately. You will not face retaliation for reporting. You may also file a complaint with the NY Division of Human Rights at (888) 392-3644.

Drug & Alcohol

Zero tolerance on job sites. Employees may be subject to testing after any workplace incident. Reporting to work impaired is grounds for immediate termination.

Company Property

  • Company trucks, equipment, and tools are for company use only.
  • Report any damage or malfunction immediately.
  • Return all company property upon separation.

Social Media & Clients

  • Do not post client addresses, property photos, or personal information without permission.
  • Job site photos for marketing require client consent.
  • Represent the company professionally at all times.

Termination

  • Employment is at-will. Either party may terminate at any time.
  • Final paycheck will be issued by the next regular payday per NY law.
  • Return all company property, keys, and PPE on your last day.

Workers' Compensation

All employees are covered by Workers' Compensation insurance through NYSIF. If you are injured on the job, report it to Doug immediately and seek medical attention. All medical bills and lost wages are covered. Do not delay reporting — late reports can delay your benefits.
