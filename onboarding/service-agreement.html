1. Scope of Work

Second Nature Tree Service LLC ("Contractor") agrees to perform the tree work described in the attached quote at the property listed above. Work includes only what is explicitly listed in the quote — any additional work requires a separate written agreement.

2. Payment Terms

  • A deposit of 50% is due upon signing to schedule the work.
  • Remaining balance is due upon completion of work.
  • Payment accepted: cash, check, credit card, or online via Stripe.
  • Invoices unpaid after 30 days are subject to a 1.5% monthly late fee.

3. Scheduling

Work will be scheduled within 2-4 weeks of deposit receipt, weather permitting. Contractor will provide 24-48 hours advance notice of the scheduled date. Rain, wind, or unsafe conditions may require rescheduling at no additional cost.

4. Access & Preparation

  • Client will provide clear access to the work area for trucks and equipment.
  • Client will remove vehicles, outdoor furniture, and fragile items from the work zone.
  • Client will confine pets during work hours.
  • If access is blocked on the scheduled date, a trip charge may apply.

5. Cleanup

All debris from the contracted work will be removed from the property unless otherwise noted in the quote. The site will be raked clean. Wood may be left on-site for the client if requested.

6. Stump Grinding

If stump grinding is included, the stump will be ground to approximately 6-8 inches below grade. Grindings will be used to fill the hole unless removal is specified. Contractor is not responsible for underground utilities, irrigation lines, or septic systems unless their location is disclosed in advance.

7. Liability & Insurance

  • Contractor carries General Liability insurance and Workers' Compensation.
  • Certificates of Insurance are available upon request.
  • Contractor is not liable for pre-existing conditions, underground utilities not disclosed by the client, or damage caused by weather events.
  • Contractor's total liability shall not exceed the contract amount.

8. Cancellation

  • Client may cancel with 48 hours notice for a full deposit refund.
  • Cancellations within 48 hours of scheduled work: deposit is forfeited.
  • If work has begun, client owes for work completed to date.

9. Permits

Client is responsible for obtaining any required tree removal permits from the municipality. Contractor can assist with permit applications if needed (additional fee may apply).

10. Warranty

Contractor warrants workmanship for 30 days. This does not cover natural events (storms, disease, pest damage) or conditions beyond Contractor's control.

11. Entire Agreement

This agreement and the attached quote constitute the entire agreement between the parties. Changes must be in writing and signed by both parties.
