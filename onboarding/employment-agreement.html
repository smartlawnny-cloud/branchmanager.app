1. At-Will Employment

Your employment with Second Nature Tree Service LLC ("Company") is at-will. Either you or the Company may end the employment relationship at any time, for any lawful reason, with or without cause or notice. Nothing in this agreement creates a guarantee of continued employment.

2. Duties & Expectations

  • You agree to perform the duties of your assigned position to the best of your ability.
  • You will follow all company safety policies, OSHA regulations, and crew lead instructions.
  • You will wear all required PPE at all times on job sites.
  • You will arrive at the truck yard by the scheduled call time, ready to work.
  • You will accurately track your hours using the Branch Manager time clock.

3. Confidentiality

During and after your employment, you agree not to disclose or use for personal benefit any confidential company information, including but not limited to:

  • Client lists, contact information, and property details
  • Pricing, quotes, and profit margins
  • Business strategies, vendor relationships, and trade methods
  • Employee pay rates and personnel information

4. Non-Solicitation

For 12 months after leaving the Company, you agree not to:

  • Solicit or contact Company clients for competing tree services
  • Recruit or hire Company employees for a competing business

This does not prevent you from working in the tree care industry — only from targeting Company clients and employees.

5. Company Property

  • All tools, equipment, PPE, uniforms, keys, and vehicles remain Company property.
  • You will return all Company property on your last day of employment.
  • Failure to return property may result in deductions as permitted by NY law.

6. Use of Phones & Photos

  • Personal phone use is limited to breaks only. No phone use while operating equipment.
  • Job site photos may be taken for Company documentation (before/after, progress).
  • Do not post client addresses, property photos, or identifiable information on personal social media without written client consent.

7. Drug & Alcohol Policy

The Company maintains a zero-tolerance drug and alcohol policy on all job sites and while operating Company vehicles or equipment. Reporting to work impaired or possessing controlled substances on a job site is grounds for immediate termination.

8. Workers' Compensation

You are covered by Workers' Compensation insurance through NYSIF from your first day. All work-related injuries must be reported to your crew lead and Doug Brown immediately — no matter how minor. Delayed reporting may affect your benefits.

9. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from this agreement will be resolved through good-faith discussion. If unresolved, disputes will be submitted to mediation in Westchester County, NY before any legal action.

10. Entire Agreement

This agreement, together with the Employee Handbook and any applicable Company policies, constitutes the entire employment agreement. This agreement supersedes any prior verbal or written promises. Changes must be in writing and signed by both parties.
