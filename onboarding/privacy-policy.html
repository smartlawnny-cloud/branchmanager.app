Privacy Policy

Branch Manager by Second Nature Tree Service LLC
Last updated: April 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Branch Manager collects information necessary to manage field service operations:

  • Employee data: Name, phone, email, pay rate, time entries, job assignments
  • Client data: Name, address, phone, email, service history, quotes, invoices
  • Job data: Addresses, descriptions, photos, crew assignments, completion status
  • Device data: Location (for job site directions), camera (for job photos), push notification tokens

2. How We Use Information

  • Schedule and dispatch crew to job sites
  • Create quotes and invoices for clients
  • Process payroll through Gusto
  • Send appointment reminders and follow-up emails
  • Track time and attendance for employees
  • Generate business reports and insights

3. Data Storage

Data is stored in:

  • Supabase (cloud database) — hosted in US-West (Oregon), encrypted at rest
  • Local device storage — for offline access and caching
  • Gusto — payroll data processed by Gusto Inc. per their privacy policy
  • Stripe — payment processing per Stripe's privacy policy
  • SendGrid — email delivery per Twilio SendGrid's privacy policy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe) to process client payments
  • Email providers (SendGrid) to send quotes, invoices, and reminders
  • Payroll provider (Gusto) to process employee pay
  • Law enforcement if required by law

5. Data Retention

  • Employee records: Duration of employment + 30 years (OSHA requirement)
  • Payroll records: 6 years (NY State requirement)
  • Client records: Retained indefinitely for service history
  • You may request deletion of your data by contacting us

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), row-level security policies on our database, session timeouts, and password hashing.

8. Children

Branch Manager is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
