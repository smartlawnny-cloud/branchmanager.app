## Description

Branch Manager is the all-in-one field service management app built specifically for tree care and landscaping businesses. Replace expensive SaaS subscriptions with a powerful tool that handles everything from client management to payroll -- right from your phone.

MANAGE YOUR ENTIRE OPERATION

- Schedule and dispatch jobs with a drag-and-drop calendar
- Track crew timesheets with clock in/out, overtime alerts, and weekly approvals
- Create and send professional quotes on-site with voice-to-quote dictation
- Generate invoices and accept payments in the field
- Manage 500+ clients with full contact history, notes, and photos

BUILT FOR THE FIELD

- Take before/after photos and attach them directly to jobs
- Get turn-by-turn directions to job sites with one tap
- View today's schedule at a glance from the home screen
- Works offline -- queued actions sync automatically when you reconnect
- Push notifications keep your crew informed of schedule changes

QUOTES AND INVOICES

- Build line-item quotes with your full product and service catalog
- Clients can view and approve quotes through a secure portal
- Convert approved quotes to jobs and invoices with one tap
- Track payment status, send reminders, and record partial payments
- Automatic follow-ups for outstanding quotes and overdue invoices

CREW AND PAYROLL

- Role-based access for owners, crew leads, and crew members
- Weekly timesheet review and approval workflow
- Overtime calculation and PTO tracking
- Payroll summary reports ready for your accountant or Gusto integration
- Employee profiles with pay rates, schedules, and work history

REPORTS AND INSIGHTS

- Revenue dashboard with year-over-year comparison
- Job pipeline metrics from leads to completed work
- Expense tracking and profitability analysis
- Search across all clients, jobs, quotes, and invoices instantly

Branch Manager is proudly built by Second Nature Tree Service in Peekskill, NY. We use it every day to run our own tree care business -- so you know it works in the real world.
